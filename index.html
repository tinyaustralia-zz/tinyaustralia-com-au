Tiny Australia are a community of independent seekers that have chosen a path of deeper connection and purpose. Our philosophy is seamingly simple; by embracing minimalism (living with less1), we become more joyful, abundant, and generous (the counter-intuitive nature of life). On one hand we are blessed with the exciting progression of technological advancements, while counter-intuitively, we are struggling more and more with depression and anxiety. Why is this?

Inspired by the ancient practice of Vipassana meditation and moved by the adventurous nature of tiny-house living, we've been working hard creating a new kind of wellness centre, a meditation retreat that offers guests an experience of self-awareness, uninterupted nature, and minimalist living in a comfortable and cosy tiny-house, without distractions. Our aspirations to build such a place are gradually coming to fruition and while there is plenty of work ahead of us to secure real-estate and partner with tiny-home builders, we are heavily focussed on achieving our completion goal by the end of 2021.
