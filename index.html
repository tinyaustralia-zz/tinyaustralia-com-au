Project GEM (Gratitude, Empathy, and Mindfulness), is a tiny-house community and development project with plans to create a meditation retreat in the heart of Queensland nature. Inspired by the ancient practice of Vipassana meditation, and drawing upon the minimalist way of life, the retreat will offer its guests various activities focussed on mental wellbeing, relaxation, and connection.
