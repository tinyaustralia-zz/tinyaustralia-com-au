Tiny Australia are a community of independent seekers that have chosen a path of deeper connection and purpose. Our philosophy is seamingly simple; by embracing minimalism (living with less1), we become more joyful, abundant, and generous (the counter-intuitive nature of life). On one hand we are blessed with the exciting progression of technological advancements, while counter-intuitively, we are struggling more and more as a nation with depression and anxiety than ever before.

"There are two ways to be rich: One is by acquiring much, and the other is by desiring little."
Jackie French Koller, 1948

Inspired by the ancient practice of Vipassana meditation and moved by the adventurous nature of tiny-house living, we've been working hard creating a new kind of wellness centre, a meditation retreat that offers guests an experience of self-awareness, uninterupted nature, and minimalist tiny-house living in comfort, without distractions. Our aspirations to build such a place are gradually coming to fruition and while there is plenty of work ahead of us to secure real-estate and partner with tiny-home builders, we are dedicated to achieving completion by the end of 2021.
