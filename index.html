Tiny Australia

"There are two ways to be rich: One is by acquiring much, and the other is by desiring little."

Tiny Australia is a development project with plans to build a tiny-house village and mindfulness retreat in the heart of nature. Inspired by the ancient practice of Vipassana meditation, and drawing upon the minimalist way of life, the retreat will offer its guests various activities focussed on mental wellbeing, relaxation, and connection.
