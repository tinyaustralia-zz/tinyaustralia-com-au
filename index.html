Tiny Australia is a new kind of wellness centre, a tiny-house experience focussed on helping to develop self-awareness. A community of independent seekers whom have chosen a path of deeper connection and purpose.

Our philosophy is seamingly simple; by embracing minimalism (living with less1), we become more joyful, abundant, and generous (the counter-intuitive nature of life). On one hand we are blessed with the exciting progression of technological advancements, while counter-intuitively, we are struggling more and more as a nation with depression and anxiety than ever before.

Inspired by the ancient practice of Vipassana meditation and moved by the venturous nature of tiny-house living, we're working hard developing our Airbnb business to offer guests a unique experience of minimalist tiny-house living surrounded by beautifull nature, without the distractions of city life.

Our aspirations to build such a retreat are gradually coming to fruition and while there is plenty of work ahead of us to secure real-estate and partner with tiny-home builders, we are dedicated to achieving completion by the end of 2021.
