Tiny Australia is a community of tiny-house enthusiasts journeying through self-awarenes and meditation practices. Our meditation retreat will be located in the heart of Queensland nature with awe-inspiring views where guests can truely escape the hussle of every-day life and allow a more cultured environment for journeying inward.

Inspired by the ancient practice of Vipassana meditation, and drawing upon a minimalist lifestyle, the retreat will offer guests a veriety of mindfulness activities focussed on mental wellbeing, relaxation, and connection. Although our retreat is still in early development, we are excited to have your visit our website where we'll be posting regular updates on progress.
