Tiny Australia

We're working hard creating Australia's first tiny-home village and mindfulness retreat.
Set in the heart of Queensland, inspired by the ancient practice of Vipassana meditation, and drawing upon the minimalist lifestyle, Tiny Australia will offer guests an escape into nature, while enjoying various activities focussed around mindfulness and wellbeing.
