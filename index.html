... a place to dissolve ego, and discover absolute truth.

"There are two ways to be rich: One is by acquiring much, and the other is by desiring little." Jackie French Koller, 1948

Tiny Australia are a community of independent seekers that have chosen a path of deeper connection and purpose. Our philosophy is seamingly simple; by embracing minimalism (living with less1), we become more joyful, abundant, and generous (the counter-intuitive nature of life). On one hand we are blessed with the exciting progression of technological advancements, while counter-intuitively, we are struggling more and more with depression and anxiety. Why is this?

Inspired by the ancient practice of Vipassana meditation and moved by the adventurous nature of tiny-house living, we have been working hard at creating a new kind of wellness centre, a meditation retreat that offers guests an experience of self-awareness, without all the distractions, yet surrounded nature while living out of a tiny-house for a while. Although our aspirations to manifest such a dream are slowing bearing fruit, we are heavily focussed on achieving this goal over the course of the next few years, with an estimated completion date of late 2021.
